We work for a sustainable future

About Us

Here at Just Vegan we have been inspired by the continuous scientific research being done to show the positive impact a plant-based lifestyle has on our health and the world around us. Our brand stands for food that reduces the negative environmental impact, saves animal lives, improves health and vitality, and supports wellbeing. This is why we developed ready to eat plant based products to bring convenience to consumers who want to have a healthier diet but don’t have the time to meal plan. Our aim is to successfully meet the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) and contribute to a healthier and more sustainable future for all, and this is how we are doing exactly that:

No poverty and zero hunger

At current there are 1.1 Billion people living in extreme poverty, yet there is enough food on earth to feed 1.5x the global population. PETA has shown a study that demonstrates if we fed grains to humans rather than livestock world poverty and hunger would virtually disappear.

Good health and well-being

Plant-based eating has been proven to not only support your mental health, but also reduce the risk of developing heart disease, diabetes, obesity and certain cancers.

Clean water and sanitation

Animal agriculture is said to be one of the main causes of water issues in the world. Not only is this industry polluting the waterways, studies now show it can take more than 4,000 gallons of water to feed the average meat-eater, and only 300 gallons to feed a typical vegan.

Decent work and economic growth

With veganism set to grow by up to 10% each year, it is a big player in the economic contribution sector and is set to reach approximately US$24.3 Billion by 2026.

Responsible consumption and production

We’ve done the research and in order to be a responsible consumer you have to know where you food is coming from and what it is impacting on. That is why we are choosing to market products that will help the world rather than harm it.

Climate action

15% of total greenhouse gas emissions come from the animal agriculture industry. To meet climate change targets there needs to be a reduction in meat consumption, and a promotion of meat alternatives like Just Vegan.

Life below water and land

Approximately 72 billion land animals are killed every year, with 37 - 120 Billion fish killed on commercial farms, and a staggering 2.7 trillion of our fish and water life killed in the wild annually. We aim to save lives and reduce the potential extinction of our fellow sentient beings.

Peace and justice

Our vision is to reduce the impact on all forms of violence, exploitation and death everywhere. We believe in justice and all beings having a right to it, that is why we chose to make food that inspires compassion.

Ingredients

Proteins

Pea • Mung Bean • Fava Bean • Brown Rice

By using a variety of plant proteins, our products deliver greater or equal levels of protein than their animal-based counterparts.

Fats

Cocoa Butter • Coconut Oil • Sunflower Oil • Canola Oil

Our fats create juicy plant-based burgers, beef, and sausages with less saturated fat than their animal counterparts.

Minerals

Calcium • Iron • Salt • Potassium Chloride

From taste to function, these minerals deliver the nutrients we expect from meat – like iron – and wake up our taste buds.

Flavors and colors

Beet Juice Extract • Apple Extract • Natural Flavors

Our natural flavors and fruit and vegetable-based colors deliver a meaty taste and cooking experience.
