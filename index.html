About Us

At Just Vegan, we know we can feed the planet in a more compasionate, sustainable and healthier way. Our mission is to create delicious plant-based meat products and more. We want to help you:

Improve your health

Adopting a plant-based diet can lower the risk and reduce symptoms of certain chronic diseases like diabetes, heart disease, cancer and more.

Protect the planet

Sustainable eating habits can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions, water consumption and land used for factory farming, which are all factors in global warming and environmental degradation.

Fight against animal cruelty

While eating a plant-based diet, you won't contribute in the slaughter of billions of animals each year after living all their lives in horrific conditions.
